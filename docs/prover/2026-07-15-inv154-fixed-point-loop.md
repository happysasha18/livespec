## Outcomes — senior fold, 08:2x 15.07.2026 (INV-154 clause + index row rewritten, PRODUCT_SPEC.md lines 442 / 1828; register-lint clean on both)

- **Finding 1 (defect) — FOLDED.** The clause now names two resting states apart: it CONVERGES when a round surfaces no grouping or difference beyond those already declared/decided; it WAITS when a round's only findings are unanswered questions. A waiting round does not count toward the cap, so a slow human never trips it. Converged and waiting are no longer indistinguishable.
- **Finding 2 (defect) — FOLDED.** The strict-shrink termination guarantee is dropped. The clause now states termination is not guaranteed (a declaration can re-partition into fresh groupings, a prover fix can add elements, so undeclared groupings can rise as well as fall); convergence is the ordinary case and the cap is the liveness guarantee.
- **Finding 3 (defect) — FOLDED.** Added: surfacing at the cap holds no landing — like every design-review finding it is a recommendation or a question, and the landing proceeds with the unsettled groupings recorded. The title now reads "surface to the human on non-convergence without holding the landing."
- **Finding 4 (defect) — FOLDED.** Grouping and element are now used apart, with the definition inline: an element is a thing a person acts on; a declaration adds a grouping over existing elements; a prover fix may add a new element; the loop re-reads for both. The failure-cause wording matches ("spawns new elements or groupings").
- **Finding 5 (recommendation) — FOLDED in substance.** The clause names the net (the design review counts its own progressing rounds and stops at the cap) and defines the round unit; the cross-landing async is covered by the wait-does-not-advance rule. The per-pass counter lifespan/reset is carried into the design-review node's loop seam in ARCHITECTURE.md (this landing's downstream step).
- **Finding 6 (recommendation) — FOLDED.** A round is now defined: one prover re-read of the changed part followed by one design-review re-read over the current spec.
- **Finding 7 (recommendation) — FOLDED.** The cause reading is marked best-effort ("its best reading of the cause ... best-effort, since a spec's self-consistency is not decidable"). The three named causes stand as the common cases under a best-effort read.

## Strongest finding (two sentences)

The loop halts on "a full round [that] adds no new class sentence and no new decided sentence," but a round in which the human has simply not yet answered the open design-review questions produces exactly that same emptiness — the INV-142 held-unanswered state the clause itself calls "a natural stop" — so the test cannot tell a genuinely converged loop from a stalled one. It will therefore declare a false fixed point the moment the human pauses (leaving real divergences unsurfaced), or, if the rest is meant not to count, fire the cap on a merely slow human and mislabel the wait as "non-convergence" with a misdiagnosed cause.
