## The strongest finding (finding 1), both objects in hand

`INV-138` (:1780) makes the viewport a **ranged and banded quantity** and holds every layout guarantee to
completeness across its bands: a guarantee holds "on every viewport" or names the band it scopes to, and a
band-scoped guarantee draws the standing question about the other bands. That enforcement is band-general, so
it is complete by construction — it already reaches a portrait-tall or ultra-wide band no one has named yet.
The author-side twin, the standard-facet sweep, does not share that shape. It enumerates two specific viewport
bands: **facet #1** "layout on a phone or narrow window" (:376), the original width-band, and **facet #9** the
orientation / short-viewport band (:384), added by row 367. The very incident that bore facet #9 was a fixed
width-enumeration that read a rotated phone as not-a-phone and printed the caption over the picture; the fix
added another fixed band beside the first. So the sweep still enumerates bands one at a time, and the next band
to bite — a tall portrait window, an ultra-wide monitor, a foldable's half-open aspect — is not on the list for
the author to answer, even though INV-138's enforcement would catch a guarantee that stayed silent on it.
INV-138 itself names facet #9 "the short-viewport facet the author-side of this same lens", which says the
intent is a band-general facet, worn here as one named band.

**Recommended default: fold facet #1 and facet #9 into one band-general facet** — "every layout-bearing
feature states its layout across the viewport as a banded quantity (narrow, wide, short, tall, and any band the
design meets), or names the bands it scopes to", the author-side mirror of INV-138's band-general lens. This is
a confident recommendation, not an ask: INV-138 already decides that the viewport is banded and complete across
its bands, so the facet's granularity is derivable from the declared lens [INV-121] and no human fork is owed
on the concept; the wording is the only open part, and the enforcement floor stands whichever way the facet is
worded. Queue it as a taste call on the exact band words. If the author prefers to keep specific band-facets
(each band its own line reads more concretely to a drafter), the alternative is a decided sentence that the
facet list enumerates bands by incident on purpose while INV-138's lens holds the completeness, so the pairing
is recorded rather than left as a silent granularity gap.
