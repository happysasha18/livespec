# Inbox wish — the feel pass, and defaults that accumulate into an unfinished product

From the tlvphoto window, 2026-07-06 ~10:10. Companion evidence to the intake-questions gap already
queued (row 108, filed from Alexander's five night comments); this file adds the half row 108 does not
cover. Alexander's morning verdict, paraphrased: features don't get finished AS features — animations
read not-smooth, an affordance ships ugly, the whole thing lacks a premium feel; he suspects the method
never asks the adoption questions.

## The gap, in two parts

1. **Verify-by-deed checks "works", never "feels".** The product kind's step 8 is "open the real
   artifact, eyes on it" — in practice the eyes confirm it renders and behaves; nobody is tasked with
   watching the MOTION (easing, duration, choreography) or judging an affordance's craft against the
   approved prototype's bar. tlvphoto evidence: reflow/unfold/exit transitions shipped green and read
   cheap to him; the arrow-in-circle open-work button shipped through the full pipeline and he called it
   ugly on sight. Green suite + eyes-on ≠ premium.

2. **Taste [default]s accumulate unreviewed.** The [default]-tag mechanism is right for velocity (the
   lane never blocks), but nothing closes the loop: tlvphoto is carrying 8+ defaults "riding to his
   review" across two landings (skip copy, exit form, hang rhythm, reflow speed, hover halo, wordmark,
   root card, default hang). A product whose every taste call is an open default reads 80%-finished
   everywhere — which is exactly his complaint.

## Proposed shape (the pack decides the form)

- **A visitor-walk / feel pass as a named part of step 8 for the product kind:** walk the WHOLE journey
  as the person would (first visit · return visit · cross-entry · from-any-point navigation · exits),
  watching feel — motion quality, affordance craft, dead ends — against the approved prototype as the
  bar; findings become rows, not vibes. (The journey-questions half at INTAKE is row 108's side; this is
  the verify-time side.)
- **Defaults close at the landing that ships them:** a landing report lists its [default]s and the NEXT
  human review resolves them (one decision page); a default may not survive two landings unreviewed —
  otherwise they compound into "не допилено".
