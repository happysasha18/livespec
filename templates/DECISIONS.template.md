# Decisions on record — what the pack believes you decided

TOUCHPOINT-KIND: decision-readback
DECISION-RECORD

This is the read-back surface (SPEC INV-207). Every line under **On record** is a decision the pack
has written down as YOURS, each naming the exchange it came from — at minimum a date — so you can go
back and check it. Read it on your own clock and strike anything you never said: move it under
**Struck** with a one-line note. A struck line is retracted, never deleted. The pack renders this file
for you through `scripts/render-doc.py` when you ask. [[teach]]

The rule the gate (`guardrails/check-authority-anchor.py`) holds: a decision recorded as yours must
name its exchange. A line with no exchange is a challengeable judgment moved into the one slot nothing
questions — the defect this surface exists to catch. [[wait]]
